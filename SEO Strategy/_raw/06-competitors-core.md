## Findings (numbered; each: finding, evidence, SEO impact, recommended fix, priority P0–P3)

1. **inkedup.id is absent from all 6 core commercial/safety SERPs.** Evidence: WebSearch result sets for all 6 queries (recorded in Data tables) — zero inkedup.id URLs in any returned result. Impact: total organic invisibility for every revenue-relevant query; competitors capture 100% of this demand. Fix: the content/schema/indexation program outlined in findings 4–8 below; treat as the central commercial gap this audit must close. **P1** (becomes P0 if finding 2 confirms an indexation failure).

2. **Possible indexation failure: `site:inkedup.id` returned no results.** Evidence: WebSearch backend response "No search results were found for the given query" (HTTP 404 no_results), 2026-07-17. Impact: if confirmed in Google, nothing can rank regardless of content quality. Caveat: this is a third-party search API, not Google — a no-result here is suggestive, not conclusive. Fix: confirm in Google Search Console (Coverage/Pages report + URL Inspection) and a browser `site:` query before escalation. **P0 if confirmed; currently UNVERIFIED.**

3. **Brand-name collision: unrelated businesses occupy brand-adjacent searches.** Evidence: search `inkedup.id bali tattoo` returned "Inked Up Tattoo Parlour Bali" listings on tattooswizard.com and tattoo.guide (a different, pre-existing Bali studio) and inkedupsouthampton.com (UK studio) — inkedup.id itself did not appear. Impact: even navigational/brand demand leaks to a namesake competitor and directories; future brand searches will be contested. Fix: consistent brand entity signals (GBP, sameAs, press/social profiles), and monitor brand SERP; consider "InkedUp Bali concierge" as the disambiguating phrase in titles. **P1**.

4. **The ranking bar is deep, first-person, studio-authored guides (1,800–2,900 words) — InkedUp has no comparable assets.** Evidence: FetchURL of Hustle Ink's "Getting a Tattoo in Bali" guide (~2,900 words: price table IDR/AUD/USD, named artists per style, hygiene checklist, timing/aftercare sections, 8-question FAQ) ranks top for both `tattoo prices bali` and `is it safe to get a tattoo in bali`; Social Ink House's owner-voice safety guide (~1,800 words: "7 years, 2,000+ clients", named ink brands, autoclave specifics, named hospitals Siloam/BIMC) ranks #2 for the safety query. Impact: Google is rewarding demonstrated first-hand experience (E-E-A-T), not generic copy; thin or generic pages (e.g. Secret Arts' ~700-word first-time guide) rank but below the deep guides. Fix: publish people-first guides from InkedUp's unique vantage (vetted-artist concierge): prices, safety, artist-selection, aftercare-in-the-tropics — first-person, specific, checkable. **P1** (strategy), individual page gaps **P2**.

5. **Review proof + AggregateRating schema is standard among winners; InkedUp shows neither.** Evidence: curl of bloodlinetattoobali.com raw HTML shows `TattooParlor` + `AggregateRating` ld+json and on-page "5.0★ · 519+ Reviews on Google"; tattoobali.com also ships `AggregateRating`; Hustle Ink claims "130+ five-star Google reviews" in copy. business.ts contains no review data. Impact: unsupported trust claims are a P1-class audit issue; review count is also a visible CTR lever in this SERP set. Fix: build Google Business Profile review velocity, surface real review counts on-page, add AggregateRating only when real. **P1**.

6. **Transparent pricing is a proven ranking weapon in this market.** Evidence: finelinetattoobali.com prices page (flat tiers: 1M IDR minimum, 2.4M/hr, 15M day session, visual per-band examples, `Offer`/`OfferCatalog`/`AggregateOffer` ld+json via curl) and Hustle Ink's price table rank #1–#3 for `tattoo prices bali`; by contrast Celebrity Ink Kuta shows no prices ("clear quote after consultation") and ranks lower for the price query. Impact: price-intent searchers are served by whoever publishes numbers; hiding prices cedes the query entirely. Fix: publish an indicative price matrix (by size/complexity, multi-currency) even if final quotes are per-design; mark up with Offer schema. **P2**.

7. **Competitors' schema stacks exceed InkedUp's — and InkedUp's live JSON-LD carries placeholder identity data.** Evidence: curl ld+json extraction — tattoobali.com: TattooParlor+AggregateRating+OpeningHours+ContactPoint; tententattoo.com: TattooParlor+GeoCoordinates+OpeningHours; tnttattoobali.com: LocalBusiness+"Tattoo Studio"; hustleink: LocalBusiness+Article+FAQ+AggregateRating; cangguinkclub blog: BlogPosting+FAQPage+Person. inkedup.id raw HTML has one static `TattooParlor` block but its fields come from business.ts, where WhatsApp is placeholder `6281234567890` (business.ts:12, marked TODO) and legalName "InkedUp Bali" is unconfirmed (business.ts:9, TODO). Also: 0 `<h1>` in inkedup.id raw HTML (CSR shell) vs every competitor serving server-rendered H1. Impact: placeholder contact data in machine-readable markup is a consistency/trust risk (NAP mismatch once real number exists elsewhere); CSR-only H1/content adds rendering dependency competitors don't have. Fix: populate real business data before any schema expansion (cross-ref: technical audit lane for CSR/rendering). **P1**.

8. **Publisher listicles, not directories, are the third competitive force.** Evidence: finnsbeachclub.com "16 Best Tattoo Studios & Artists" (~1,900 words + 8 FAQs, `Article` schema) appears for `tattoo bali`, `tattoo artist bali`, AND `tattoo shop bali`; traveloka.com listicle appears for `tattoo bali`. No TripAdvisor/Yelp/Fresha/Tattoodo in any top organic set; tattoo.guide/tattooswizard surfaced only for the brand-name search. Impact: a curated "best of Bali" list is proven winnable by a local publisher; entries are shallow (~60–90 words each) — beatable by deeper, first-hand vetting content. Fix: InkedUp's marketplace position allows a genuinely useful "how to choose / vetted studios & artists in Bali" hub; also pursue inclusion in Finns/Traveloka-style lists as a citation/PR play. **P2** (intel + opportunity).

9. **WhatsApp is the universal booking path — parity is easy, but InkedUp's number is a placeholder.** Evidence: Hustle Ink (+62 812 2525-2698) and Social Ink House (+62 877 8094 9981) publish WhatsApp in ranking content; Bloodline is "WhatsApp for availability" only; InkedUp's wa.me links all derive from placeholder `6281234567890` (business.ts:12, 30-33). Impact: any user clicking through would hit a dead number — conversion-critical, and a trust-claim mismatch (cross-lane with conversion audit). Fix: real number before launch/marketing; keep WhatsApp-first CTA (matches market norm). **P1**.

10. **The mobile/villa concierge angle is unoccupied in all 6 SERPs.** Evidence: every ranking competitor is a fixed-address single studio or chain (addresses in Kuta/Legian/Seminyak/Canggu/Ubud recur in fetched copy); none offer in-villa service; inkedup.id's own positioning ("we come to your villa", meta description + JSON-LD) has no SERP competitor. Impact: differentiation opportunity, not a gap — but it also means core head terms are contested by entrenched studios and InkedUp must win on trust + content, or target adjacent intent ("tattoo at villa bali", "mobile tattoo bali") where competition is near-zero. Fix: see differentiation list below. **P2**.

11. **Tactical competitor weaknesses a new platform can exploit.** Evidence: Secret Arts homepage served an **empty `<title>`** tag (curl); tnttattoobali.com homepage HTML is **1.9 MB** (curl byte count — performance liability); Celebrity Ink's Kuta location page ships only WebPage/Breadcrumb schema — no LocalBusiness/TattooParlor on the location page (curl) — and its FAQ text appears duplicated in extracted content; Finns listicle entries are one-paragraph shallow; most studios publish no prices at all. Impact: incumbents are beatable on technical hygiene, pricing transparency, and content depth simultaneously. Fix: none needed beyond executing findings 4–6. **P3** (intel).

## Synthesis

**What Google currently rewards for these queries:** (1) deep first-person guides (1,800–2,900 words) written by identifiable studio operators with named artists, concrete IDR/AUD/USD price tables, and hygiene specifics; (2) transparent flat-rate pricing with Offer markup; (3) visible review counts backed by AggregateRating schema; (4) TattooParlor/LocalBusiness schema with geo + hours on server-rendered pages with proper H1s; (5) FAQ sections (FAQPage markup on informational posts); (6) publisher listicles from locally authoritative brands (Finns); (7) WhatsApp as the default conversion path. Notably absent from these SERPs: global directories (TripAdvisor/Yelp/Fresha/Tattoodo) and any marketplace/concierge model.

**Minimum bar a competing page must meet:** server-rendered content with keyword-aligned title+H1; ≥1,500 words of people-first, experience-based copy per intent; named artists with portfolios (healed work preferred); explicit hygiene protocol; indicative prices; real review proof; FAQ + TattooParlor/Offer/FAQPage schema as appropriate; WhatsApp booking; fast mobile delivery. Anything thinner ranks only where intent is uncontested.

**Differentiation opportunities for InkedUp (concierge/marketplace model):**
1. Own the unoccupied "mobile / in-villa tattoo" intent cluster — zero competitors serve it in any observed SERP.
2. Publish the vetting standard: a concrete, checkable artist-verification rubric (sterilization audit, portfolio review, healed-work requirement) — turns "verified artists" from a claim into evidence no single studio can match.
3. Platform-wide instant indicative pricing across artists/styles (vs. per-studio quotes) with multi-currency tables.
4. Mandatory healed-work gallery (4–8 weeks post-session) for every listed artist — only Bloodline/Hustle even mention healed work today.
5. Neutral, non-self-serving safety/choice content ("how to choose any studio in Bali, including competitors") — the E-E-A-T inverse of every studio's "we're the best" page.
6. Documented mobile sterile-setup (photo-documented kit, single-use consumables brought to the villa) as a safety-content asset unique to the model.
7. Aftercare-as-a-service angle: scheduled day-2/day-7 WhatsApp check-ins — a service loop fixed studios don't offer, and a content asset for tropical-healing queries.
8. Per-district coverage pages for the six areaServed districts with villa/hotel coverage and travel-time detail — competitors each own one neighborhood; InkedUp can legitimately cover all.
