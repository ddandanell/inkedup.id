## Findings (numbered)

1. **inkedup.id appears in ZERO observed SERPs, including branded-adjacent queries.** `site:inkedup.id` returned "no results" (backend caveat), and `inkedup bali tattoo` surfaces a UK namesake (Inked Up Southampton) and generic #inkedup hashtag content. Every commercial query observed is owned by competitors/listicles. Evidence: WebSearch runs above. Impact: no organic visibility baseline; brand term itself is contested by a same-name UK studio and a generic hashtag — brand SERP must be secured early (GBP, consistent NAP, brand schema). Fix: verify indexation in GSC; build homepage/brand assets; earn listicle inclusions. **Priority: P1.**

2. **"Tattoo concierge" positioning term is unclaimed in observed SERPs** — quoted search surfaced only generic villa/home-service results (Come To You Tattoo, Hendric Shinigami guide). Impact: InkedUp can own the category term with homepage + /how-it-works, while competitors fight over "studio". Fix: keep "Mobile Tattoo Concierge" in title/H1; reinforce via schema + consistent phrasing. **Priority: P2.**

3. **Service-model cluster (mobile/villa tattoo) is thin but contested on trust.** Direct evidence of demand: cometoyoutattoo.com (min 2.5M IDR), Hendric Shinigami home-service guide (Jan 2026), tattooholidaybali.com packages, plus villa-delivered aftercare (Trishnanda). BUT Two Guns' Bali guide explicitly says villa/hotel tattoos are a "biggest mistake" on hygiene grounds — the exact objection to InkedUp's model. Impact: the cluster is winnable ONLY with strong hygiene proof content. Fix: build one /mobile-tattoo-bali hub + hard-hitting /safety page with sterile-protocol evidence. **Priority: P1.**

4. **Existing 8 location pages target "Mobile Tattoo Artist in {Area}" — they miss the head local terms** ("tattoo canggu", "tattoo studio seminyak") that studio homepages and directories dominate. Legian and Denpasar have NO pages (Legian SERP is active: Hustle Ink, Mex, Rose, Two Guns; Denpasar thin). Impact: mid-funnel local demand unaddressed; H1s skip the highest-volume local phrasing. Fix: retitle/reposition location pages to "{Area} tattoo" primary + mobile as differentiator; add /locations/legian and /locations/denpasar. **Priority: P2.**

5. **No style pages exist; competitors run style landing architecture.** Evidence: cangguinkclub.com/tattoo/japanese/, primitivetattoobali.com/mandala-tattoo-seminyak/, tropicalinkbali.com (fine-line-led brand), besttattooartistbali.com/styles/* (japanese, cover-up, fine-line comparison pages), Hustle Ink cover-up guide. Impact: high-intent style queries ("fine line tattoo bali", "cover up tattoo bali", "japanese tattoo bali") have no InkedUp landing surface. Fix: /styles/{style} hub, prioritized: fine-line, cover-up, japanese first. **Priority: P2.**

6. **Price SERP is guide-dominated with concrete numbers; InkedUp /pricing exists but must publish real ranges.** Observed benchmarks: small IDR 500K–1.5M; studio minimums IDR 300K–600K; hourly IDR 800K–3M (finelinetattoobali 2.4M/hr); full-day IDR 5M–15M; mobile minimum 2.5M IDR; cover-up +20–50%. Impact: transparency is table stakes (finelinetattoobali ranks on exactly this); /pricing without numbers loses to 5+ guide competitors. Fix: enrich /pricing with real ranges + FAQ; do NOT create a separate price blog guide (cannibalization). **Priority: P2.**

7. **Safety/info cluster: /safety and /faq exist — good foundation; aftercare content gap.** Competitors publish dedicated tropical-aftercare guides (Downunder, W Ink, Tropical Ink, Blacklow, Canggu Ink Club day-by-day) with consistent rules (no swimming 14 days; sunscreen only after 3–4 weeks; tattoo at end of trip). Impact: aftercare queries are numerous, link-worthy, and pre-empt the mobile-hygiene objection. Fix: one comprehensive /guides/tattoo-aftercare-bali (swimming/sun/flying/alcohol/infection sections) linked from /safety, /faq, booking confirmation. **Priority: P2.**

8. **Commercial-investigation queries are owned by listicles + a dedicated directory** (besttattooartistbali.com with area/style comparison pages and huge review counts; Finns "16 Best"; Bali Buddies 2026 list literally titled "Where To Get Inked-Up In Bali"). Impact: hard to outrank head "best" terms short-term; realistic play is (a) /reviews trust page, (b) "how to choose" guide, (c) get listed in those listicles. Fix: propose /reviews + /guides/how-to-choose-tattoo-studio-bali; outreach for listicle inclusion. **Priority: P2.**

9. **Partnership cluster is low-volume but real and uncontested by Bali concierges.** Evidence: Jobstreet tattoo-artist listings (Rp 8–12M/mo), Glints studio-manager/artist posts, bookanartist.co marketplace, guest-spot commission models abroad (30% at Ikonic Berlin). /studio/apply already exists. Fix: keep one application page; title it for "tattoo artist jobs/partnership Bali"; no new URLs. **Priority: P3.**

10. **Cannibalization risks (explicit):** (a) homepage vs /studios vs /artists all drifting toward "tattoo bali" — differentiate intents (brand/booking vs studio directory vs artist directory); (b) /locations/{x} ("Mobile Tattoo Artist in X") vs proposed /mobile-tattoo-bali hub vs "tattoo {x}" head terms — location pages = local intent, hub = generic service-model intent; do not let location pages and hub swap targets; (c) /pricing vs any proposed price guide — keep ONE price URL; (d) /safety vs /faq vs proposed aftercare guide — safety = is-it-safe/hygiene standards, FAQ = short answers, guide = aftercare steps; cross-link, don't duplicate; (e) /styles/{style} vs /artists/:slug — style pages curate/link to artists, must not duplicate artist portfolio content; (f) do NOT create both /mobile-tattoo-bali and /villa-tattoo-bali — same SERP, one hub. **Priority: P1 (architecture decision before build).**

11. **Adjacent opportunities observed (lower priority):** "bali tattoo ideas" inspiration queries (Downunder styles guide, Hendric "10 Big Tattoo Ideas", Mason's ideas blog) — /inspiration exists, retarget P3; geo-arbitrage country pages ("tattoo in bali from australia/netherlands" — secretartstattoo.com pattern) P3; bamboo/traditional Balinese tattoo content (Ubud Ink, Suku Suku Tatau, Sak Yant demand) — only if artists genuinely offer it, P3; German-language demand signaled by competitor copy ("tattoo shop in meiner Nähe" on finelinetattoobali.com) — owner to confirm, P3. **Priority: P3.**

12. **"Near me" behavior cannot be won with pages alone.** Competitors already optimize copy for "tattoo near me" (finelinetattoobali.com). As a mobile concierge without a walk-in studio, InkedUp's near-me/Maps presence depends on Google Business Profile (service-area business) + location pages — GBP status is unverified in repo. **Priority: P1 (OWNER-VERIFY).**
