# InkedUp Blue Book — Execution Plan

## Overview
Create the complete Blue Book for InkedUp, a premium mobile tattoo booking platform in Bali. The user's provided positioning/trust content becomes Chapter 3 (Core Positioning & Trust Framework) — the backbone of the entire document.

## Structure (20 Chapters)
1. Executive Summary
2. Market Opportunity
3. **Core Positioning & Trust Framework** (user-provided content — the backbone)
4. Customer Segments
5. City & Area SEO Structure
6. Service Page Structure
7. Artist Profile & Verification System
8. Customer Booking Flow
9. Sales-Optimized Website Structure
10. Homepage Blueprint
11. Conversion System
12. WhatsApp Sales Scripts
13. Pricing Model
14. Investor Business Model
15. Operations Manual
16. Safety & Trust System
17. SEO Content Plan
18. Blog Strategy (50 titles)
19. Competitor Analysis
20. Brand Positioning & 90-Day Launch Plan

## Execution Stages

### Stage 1 — Research
- Load: `deep-research-swarm`
- Deploy parallel research agents for:
  - Bali tattoo market + competitor landscape
  - Tourist behavior + villa service trends in Bali
  - Tattoo safety standards + legal requirements in Indonesia
  - SEO keyword research for tattoo-related searches in Bali
- Output: Validated research brief with real data

### Stage 2 — Writing
- Load: `report-writing`
- Produce all 20 chapters using the phased writing pipeline
- Feed Stage 1 research into all chapters
- User's positioning content anchors Chapter 3 and informs all other chapters
- Output: Complete Blue Book in Markdown

### Stage 3 — Formatting
- Load: `docx`
- Convert final Markdown to professional Word document
- Output: `.docx` file delivered

## Quality Standards
- Every claim marked with "must verify before publishing" unless confirmed
- Premium tone throughout — high-end concierge, never cheap
- Sales-optimized — every section moves customer closer to booking
- Specific, not generic — real operator thinking
- Tables, checklists, copy examples, SEO keywords throughout
