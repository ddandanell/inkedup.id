## 9. Unit Economics

### Marketplace Unit Economics Framework

Claim: For a marketplace, a healthy per-transaction contribution margin is generally considered to be over 25%, with above 50% being strong. The fundamental equation: (Buyer LTV + Seller LTV) > (Buyer CAC + Seller CAC). [^35^]
Source: Glencoyne — E-commerce Marketplace Unit Economics

| Scenario | Policy | Financial Impact |
|----------|--------|-----------------|
| Cancellation 48+ hours before | Deposit transferable to new date | No loss |
