## Executive Summary: Top 10 Key Findings

1. **Bali's tattoo market is estimated at USD 25-40 million annually**, derived from ~280-1,000 studios serving 6.33M international tourists and a growing domestic base. Southeast Asia's tattoo market was valued at USD 34.19M in 2024 [^26^].

2. **Bali has 280-306 registered tattoo studios**, but industry insiders estimate as many as 1,000 studios operate on the island — indicating a highly fragmented, largely unregulated market [^3^] [^33^] [^71^].

3. **"Tattourism" is a confirmed global trend**: 40% of travelers aged 18-35 have gotten a tattoo while on a trip, and over 50% of those planned their tattoo travel abroad specifically for the purpose [^194^] [^192^].

4. **Australia is Bali's #1 tattoo tourism source market**: 1.54M Australian visitors (24.78% of international arrivals), with 30% of Australians now having at least one tattoo — up from 20% in 2018 [^10^] [^156^].

5. **Bali tattoo prices are 50-70% cheaper than Australia**: A small tattoo costing AUD $150-350 in Sydney runs AUD $50-150 in Bali; medium pieces at AUD $600 in Bali would cost AUD $1,500+ in Australia [^12^] [^3^].

6. **Bali tourism has fully recovered post-pandemic**: 6.33M international visitors in 2024 exceeded the 2019 peak of 6.28M, with 2025 on track to reach 6.5M+ [^170^] [^10^].

7. **Only ONE mobile tattoo service exists in Bali** — "Come To You Tattoo" — with a minimum charge of IDR 2,500,000 (~AUD 200), confirming the mobile-to-villa gap that InkedUp would fill [^101^].

8. **Celebrity Ink is the only regional chain present** (Asia-Pacific's largest with 25+ studios), alongside ink.inc positioning as a luxury brand — but 90%+ of the market remains independent, unbranded studios [^70^] [^71^] [^2^].

9. **The global tattoo market is growing at 9.5-10.7% CAGR**, with Asia-Pacific as the fastest-growing region at 12.6% CAGR, making Bali a strategic beachhead for expansion across Southeast Asia [^26^] [^27^].

10. **Bali Tattoo Expo**, the island's premier industry event (running since 2016), reached its 6th edition in August 2024 — demonstrating market maturation and growing professionalization [^270^].
