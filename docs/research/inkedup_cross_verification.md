## CONFLICT ZONES (Disagreements between sources)

### Conflict 1: Digital Nomad Population Count
- **Dim 07**: 40,000+ digital nomads in Bali (2024)
- **Landscape scan**: 3,000+ digital nomads
- **Resolution**: The 3,000 figure likely refers to long-term residents on nomad-specific visas. The 40,000 figure includes all remote workers staying 1+ months. Both can be true depending on definition. **Status: RESOLVED** — use "40,000+ including short-term remote workers" with note on definitional variance.

### Conflict 2: Tattoo Price Ranges
- **Dim 09 (budget end)**: Studio minimum IDR 300K
- **Dim 09 (premium end)**: HBSC minimum IDR 800K, Koloni Tattoo minimum IDR 1.5M
- **Resolution**: Not a true conflict — price ranges reflect different studio tiers. Budget studios start at 300K, premium at 800K-1.5M. **Status: RESOLVED** — present as tiered pricing.

### Conflict 3: Optimal Commission Rate
- **Dim 08**: 20-25% recommended
- **Dim 09**: 15% recommended
- **Resolution**: Dim 08 bases on service marketplace benchmarks (Soothe 30%, Glamsquad 20%). Dim 09 bases on Airbnb model (15%). For a premium service with high artist value, 20% is the compromise position. **Status: RESOLVED** — recommend 20% with 0% intro period for anchor artists.

### Conflict 4: Best Timing for Tattoo During Bali Trip
- **Dim 04 (some sources)**: Get tattooed early in trip for healing time
- **Dim 04 (other sources)**: Get tattooed at end of trip to avoid activity disruption
- **Resolution**: Both have valid points. This is actually a content opportunity — InkedUp can provide definitive guidance. **Status: UNRESOLVED** — genuine disagreement in market; InkedUp should create authoritative content.
