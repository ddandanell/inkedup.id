#### 13.2.1 Villa call-out: free in Canggu/Seminyak, IDR 100-150K in Uluwatu/Jimbaran, IDR 200-300K in Ubud
#### 13.2.2 Group booking: 10% discount for 3-5 people, 15% for 6+, artist travels once serves many
#### 13.2.3 Express booking: IDR 200K premium for bookings within 48 hours
### 13.3 Platform Economics
#### 13.3.1 Commission: 20% platform fee (industry benchmark: Airbnb 15%, Soothe 30%)
#### 13.3.2 Artist payout: 80% to artist (vs 40-60% at traditional studios — massive recruitment advantage)
#### 13.3.3 Payment processing: 2.9% + flat fee, platform net margin ~15-17%
### 13.4 Deposit and Cancellation
#### 13.4.1 Deposit: 25% of estimated price, non-refundable, transferable with 48-hour notice
#### 13.4.2 Cancellation: full refund if cancelled 7+ days before, 50% refund 3-7 days, no refund <3 days
#### 13.4.3 Rescheduling: free with 48-hour notice, max 2 reschedules per booking
